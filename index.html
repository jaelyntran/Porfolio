About Me

Though I now call Los Angeles home, I was born and raised in the vibrant city of Ho Chi Minh, Vietnam. I obtained an associate's degree in Computer Science from Orange Coast College before transferring to University of California, Irvine, where I'm finishing my bachelor's degree in the same field.

Given the vast and ever-evolving nature of computer science, much of my academic journey has been a process of exploration and self-discovery as I navigated through different specialties. It wasn’t until recently that I discovered my passion for web design and databases—an ideal combination that allows me to blend my creativity, problem-solving skills, and strong preference for efficiency. By continuing to build on my backend experience in databases and web applications with front-end design, I hope to bring responsive and engaging products to life.
